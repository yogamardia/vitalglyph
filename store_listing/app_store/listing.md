## Description (4000 chars max)
VitalGlyph is an offline-first emergency medical ID app that puts your privacy first. Store critical medical information — blood type, allergies, medications, conditions, and emergency contacts — in one secure place on your device.

EMERGENCY-READY QR CODES
Generate a QR code that first responders can scan instantly to access your medical information — no internet required. Print a credit-card-sized emergency card or display it from your lock screen widget.

TRULY PRIVATE
• No accounts required
• No servers — zero network requests
• No analytics or tracking of any kind
• All data stored locally on your device

SECURE BY DESIGN
• PIN and Face ID/Touch ID app lock
• AES-256-CBC encrypted backups
• HMAC-SHA256 QR code integrity verification

FEATURES
• Multiple medical profiles for your whole family
• Blood type, allergies, medications, conditions, emergency contacts
• Emergency PDF card generation (credit-card size, printable)
• Home screen widget with QR code for instant access
• Encrypted .medid backup files — export via AirDrop, Files, or any share target
• Light and dark mode
• Full VoiceOver accessibility support
