## Full Description (4000 chars max)
VitalGlyph is an offline-first emergency medical ID app that puts your privacy first. Store critical medical information — blood type, allergies, medications, conditions, and emergency contacts — in one secure place on your device.

**Emergency-Ready QR Codes**
Generate a QR code that first responders can scan instantly to access your medical information — no internet needed. Print an emergency card or show it directly from your phone.

**Truly Private**
• No accounts required
• No servers — zero network requests
• No analytics or tracking
• All data stored locally on your device

**Secure by Design**
• PIN and biometric (fingerprint/Face ID) app lock
• AES-256-CBC encrypted backups
• HMAC-SHA256 QR code integrity verification

**Features**
• Multiple medical profiles (family members)
• Blood type, allergies, medications, conditions, emergency contacts
• Emergency PDF card generation (credit-card size)
• Home screen widget with QR code
• Encrypted .medid backup files — export, share, and restore
• Dark mode support
• Full accessibility support

**Open Source**
VitalGlyph is open source under the MPL-2.0 license. Audit the code yourself at:
https://github.com/yogamardia/vitalglyph

This app is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always call emergency services in an emergency.
