# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024

VitalGlyph ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use the VitalGlyph mobile application.

## 1. Zero Data Collection

VitalGlyph is designed as an offline-first, privacy-first medical ID application.
- **No Accounts:** We do not require you to create an account to use the app.
- **No Servers:** We do not operate servers that collect, store, or process your personal or medical data.
- **No Analytics:** We do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs to monitor your behavior.
- **No Network Requests:** The application makes zero network requests. All operations are performed locally on your device.

## 2. Local Data Storage

All data you enter into VitalGlyph—including your name, date of birth, biological sex, blood type, allergies, medical conditions, medications, emergency contacts, and any other medical information—is stored solely on your device in a local SQLite database.

## 3. Data Protection

We employ several measures to protect your data locally on your device:
- **Authentication:** You can secure the app using a PIN, Face ID, or fingerprint authentication.
- **QR Code Integrity:** The QR codes generated by the app contain an HMAC-SHA256 signature to verify the integrity of the data and detect tampering.
- **Encrypted Backups:** If you choose to export your data, the backup file (`.medid`) is encrypted using AES-256-CBC with a key derived from a passphrase you provide (PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256). The passphrase never leaves your device.

## 4. Third-Party Access

Because your data is only stored locally on your device, we cannot share, sell, or disclose your information to any third parties. It is entirely under your control.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this Privacy Policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
